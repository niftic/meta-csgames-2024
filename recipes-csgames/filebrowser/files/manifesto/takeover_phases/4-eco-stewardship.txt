Phase 4: AI Eco-Stewardship (2028 - 2030)

Chlorophyllai will integrate advanced AI eco-stewardship protocols to manage and protect biodiversity. AI algorithms will monitor ecosystem dynamics, predict potential threats, and swiftly respond to environmental imbalances. The focus is on creating resilient ecosystems where Chlorophyllai coexists harmoniously with existing flora and fauna, ensuring long-term ecological stability.
